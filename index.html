Psychology Chapter 1

Psychology is the scientific study of behavior and mental processes. In Chapter 1, we explore the origins, key approaches, and major theories in psychology. The term was derived from the Greek words 'psyche' meaning soul, and 'logos' meaning study.

Topics include:

  • The Biological Perspective
  • Cognitive Psychology
  • Behavioral Approach
  • Humanistic and Psychodynamic views

This chapter provides foundational concepts that will guide your understanding throughout the course.
